🔧 Troubleshooting Tips

Token Expiration: The system automatically refreshes tokens before they expire. If you see persistent authentication errors, use "Sign Out & Restart" above.

Automatic Sync: The scheduler runs every hour while authenticated. Check the "Auto Sync" status above to ensure it's active.

Rate Limiting: To prevent overload, manual syncs are limited to 20 per hour. The limit resets automatically.
